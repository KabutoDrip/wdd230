Hicks Law

Good Meat

Good Meat Follow hicks law of limited options by only allowing one decision to be made in a single viewport, If you wish to see the next option you scroll to the next section. The navigation dropdown also follows this principle by only providing 3 decisions to be made

White Space and Clean Design

Sennesro

Sennesro Follows the idea of white space elegantly. Looking through the webiste you see alot of nothing surrounding what is important, the imagery provided. Its clear and draws attention to their focal point.

PARC: Contrast

Dropbox

Dropbox follows the idea of contrast wonderfully, Its not shown in the screenshot well but they have multiple image cards for each different purpose the page provides. Each image card is colored differently with a contrasting text color to make the wording pop. I ultimately selected dropbox for its exhibit of knowledge of contrast.
